Oreo Cheesecake

Who doesn't like Oreos, right? Combine it with a cheesecake and you get... perfection!
This is a super easy and quick oreo cheesecake recipe that will impress anyone who tries it!

Ingredients

For Cheesecake:

  • 310g Oreos
  • 90g Unsalted Butter
  • 250g White Chocolate
  • 350ml Double Cream
  • 500g Cream Cheese
  • 120g Icing Sugar

For Decoration:

  • 250g White Chocolate
  • 150ml Double Cream
  • 8 Oreos

Instructions

  1. Crush 290g of the Oreos finely (in a processer if possible as this makes it a lot easier ðŸ˜Œ) and 20g a little more coarsely, which should be set aside.
  2. Melt butter and mix with the fine Oreo crumbs until combined.
  3. Tranfer to a 8 x 8" tin (or any appropriate tin that you can find) and press it into the base firmly before setting it aside.
  4. For the actual cheesecake, the first step is to melt the white chocolate using a microwave/stove.
  5. Whilst this cools, pour the double cream into a bowl and whisk to stiff peaks before setting it aside.
  6. In another bowl, whisk together the cream cheese and icing sugar.
  7. Pour in melted white chocolate and whisk until combined.
  8. Add the whipped cream and fold until all the components have fully combined before adding the 20g of coarse Oreo pieces.
  9. Transfer the mixture into the tin and smooth it out as best as you can.
  10. Place it in a refridgerator for a minimum of 4 hours (for the best results, leave it overnight!)
  11. For the finishing touches, melt 250g of white chocolate with the 150ml of double cream using a microwave/stove and allow it to cool for 15 minutes.
  12. Take out the cheesecake from the tin and place it on a plate, on top of a rack.
  13. Pour the white chocolate mix over the cheesecake and smooth it out.
  14. Finally, halve 8 Oreos and place them on top of the cheesecake (see above image for inspiration) before serving.
  15. Sit back and enjoy!
